FAQ Section

How many people can travel in one car?

4 participants can travel in one registered car.

What is the registration fee?

₹2,000 per car.

Is the rally safe?

Yes. The rally follows organised instructions, route discipline, safety guidelines and required permissions.

Can non-Arya Vysya members participate?

Yes, in the Open Community Category with minimum 2 Arya Vysya participants.

What happens during the rally?

Teams follow the guide map, visit heritage spots, discover answers to rally questions and complete the route.

Are registrations limited?

Yes, registrations are limited to 100 cars only.
